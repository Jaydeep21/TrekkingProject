About us

We live for the nature

With years of experience in organizing group trekking events, we have perfected the art of creating unique and unforgettable experiences for our participants. Our team of skilled and knowledgeable guides are passionate about sharing their love of the outdoors with others and ensuring that every trek is a success.

Whether you are looking to explore the wilderness with friends or make new connections with like-minded adventurers, we have the perfect trek for you. Join us on our next adventure and experience the thrill of exploring the great outdoors in a whole new way.

  • Our mountains and our location

    Canada is home to several majestic mountain ranges, including the Rocky Mountains, the Coast Mountains, and the Canadian Appalachians. These ranges boast some of the most stunning peaks in North America, such as Mount Robson, Mount Assiniboine, and Mount Logan.

  • About our treks and trips

    At Alpins, we offer a range of trekking and hiking adventures to suit every level of experience, from beginners to seasoned veterans. Our treks take you through some of the most stunning and remote landscapes in the country, allowing you to immerse yourself in nature and escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

  • About our team and guests

    We offer experienced and passionate individuals who lead the group trekking/hiking events, showcasing their expertise and commitment to providing a safe and enjoyable outdoor experience for all participants.
